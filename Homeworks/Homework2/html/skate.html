LANCE AND FRIENDS IN SUN VALLEY

For Spring 2022, Lance Mountain and the Stüssy crew head to The Pink Motel. Sitting alongside U.S Route 99 in La Tuna Canyon, California, the historic motel is well known for many legendary moments from the Powell Peralta's "The Search For Animal Chin." The film consists of a mix of new footage captured by Antosh Cimoszko and archival footage of Lance skating the historic fish-shaped pool in the 1980s.
