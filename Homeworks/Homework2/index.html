STUSSY FALL LOOKBOOK

For Fall '23, Stüssy presents a collection of enigmatic staples alongside seasonal mainstays. The collection experiments with proportions, technical elements, and iconic visuals. This unconventional approach to the familiar brings together python- patterned pants, pigment-dyed sweaters, recycled GORE-TEX® shells, and mohair club jackets, blurring the lines between novelty and daily wear.

STUSSY x LEVI'S

Stüssy & Levi’s® announced today the launch of their latest collaborative collection, set for Spring/Summer 2023. The beloved brands have shared roots in California – Stüssy was born from the Southern California surf scene in the late ’80s and early ’90s while Levi’s® was founded in San Francisco 170 years ago. 2023 marks the 150th anniversary of Levi’s® 501® Jeans, one of the most enduring icons of global style. The range from Stüssy & Levi’s® features a pair of 501® Jeans and a modified Type II Trucker Jacket – two classic Levi’s® silhouettes reimagined with Stüssy’s signature design aesthetic.

STUSSY x NIKE AIR PENNY II

Back for another round, Stüssy adds its signature to the Nike Air Penny II. Coming in two distinct colorways, a tonal fossil, and a rattan, black, and blue. The collaborative touches return with Stüssy branding on the upper and outsole. In addition, an apparel capsule collection complements the footwear. Co-branded NRG cotton sweatpants come in blue, grey, and black and feature contrasting co-branding on the thigh, are lined with fleece, and have elasticated trims. The wool pinstripe hooded zip-up jacket with matching pants, socks, a graphic heavy knit sweater and t-shirt round out the offering.
